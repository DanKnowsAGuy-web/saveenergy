About this estimate

This estimator returns a conservative range, not a projection of peak performance. Power harmonization delivers whole-facility savings on every site and is always included. Cooling and refrigeration savings come from the HVAC Optimizer and SolarCoil retrofits up to SunCore replacement. Heating savings are reached only by SunCore replacement and reflect a coefficient-of-performance comparison to existing electric heat. Refrigeration-led facilities stay cooling-dominated regardless of climate.

Actual verified savings depend on a site assessment of equipment, load profile, rate structure, and condition. Energy Plus is an Independent Energy Solutions Partner with no vendor incentive to inflate these figures. Every assumption is editable above.
